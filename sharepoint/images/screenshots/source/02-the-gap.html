The hidden gap

Your sponsor exists in Microsoft Entra.
The guest just can't see it.

When a B2B guest accepts an invitation, the sponsor relationship is already recorded in Entra — but SharePoint has no built-in way to surface it. Guests only ever see whoever sent the invitation email. We change that.
